Features

Designed for learning, not for engagement metrics.

Every feature exists to help your child build math confidence — no dark patterns, no infinite scroll, no push notifications.

🎯

Adaptive Placement

An 18-question diagnostic finds exactly where your child is — from counting to pre-algebra. No parent guesswork needed. The app starts them at the right level automatically.

💡

3-Tier Hints + Corrections

Stuck? The app shows a concrete example, then a strategy, then a worked solution. After two wrong answers, it reveals the correct answer with a step-by-step explanation. Every hint is hand-authored — predictable, age-appropriate, and always helpful.

🔊

Professional Narration

2,500+ studio-quality audio clips read every question aloud in four voice styles. Perfect for emerging readers in K-2 who can do the math but need help with the words.

⭐

Sticker Rewards

43 collectible stickers across 6 themed worlds. Every completed unit earns a new sticker for their collection — real progress they can see and feel proud of.

🔄

Spaced Review

Skills come back for review at just the right time — 1, 3, 7, 14, and 30 days later. Spacing out practice is one of the strongest evidence-based learning strategies.

🛡

Parent Dashboard

See placement results, mastery progress, weak spots, and recent session history. Everything behind a parent gate your child cannot bypass.

For Parents

Built for parents who care about privacy.

You should not have to choose between educational value and data safety. Sprout Math gives you both.

Settings are locked behind a math problem a young child cannot solve. Your child plays; you stay in control.

📈

Progress visibility

See placement results, mastery percentages, domain coverage, and the last 50 sessions with accuracy breakdowns.

Diagnostics export

Export a local diagnostics file for your records. It is never sent to a server — you decide what happens with it.
